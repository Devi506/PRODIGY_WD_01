ABOUT

In the year 1679, amidst the bustling streets of Istanbul, a young girl named Selin stumbled upon a mysterious discovery that would forever change the course of history. Legend has it that Selin, while wandering through the bustling market, encountered a peculiar bean that emitted a tantalizing aroma. Intrigued by its scent, she decided to take it home and experiment with it.
After days of trial and error, Selin finally unlocked the secret hidden within the beans. She discovered that by roasting and grinding them, she could create a rich and invigorating beverage. Excited by her newfound creation, she shared it with her friends and family, who were immediately captivated by its taste and rejuvenating effects.
Word of Selin's discovery spread like wildfire throughout the city, attracting the attention of traders and merchants from distant lands. Soon, Istanbul became a hub for the trade of this magical bean, known as "kahve" in Turkish.
As the popularity of this newfound beverage soared, so did Selin's reputation. She became known as the "Queen of Kahve," revered for her pioneering spirit and her contribution to the world of coffee. To this day, her legacy lives on, immortalized in the aromatic brew that brings people together across continents.
